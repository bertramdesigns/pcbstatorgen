# ADR Template

Replace the title below. Status is `Proposed` until ratified; flip to
`Accepted` (or another status) at sign-off time.

---

# NNNN — <Short imperative title>

**Status:** Proposed
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Superseded by:** — (or link to superseding ADR)
**Supersedes:** — (or link to superseded ADR)

## Context

What is the situation? What forces are in play? What constraints or pressures
forced this decision? Cite file:line references for every concrete claim about
the codebase. Keep this section short — the goal is to give a reader in six
months enough context to evaluate the decision, not to retell the entire
history.

## Decision

State the choice as a positive commitment. Use the active voice. One
paragraph is usually enough.

## Consequences

What becomes easier? What becomes harder? What constraints does this place
on future work? List the negative consequences honestly — a "no
consequences" section is a red flag.

## Alternatives considered

List the other options that were considered, and why each was rejected. The
goal is to make the rejected options greppable so future contributors don't
re-litigate them.
